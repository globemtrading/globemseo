WhatsApp Marketing

WhatsApp marketing uses the popular messaging app to connect with customers and promote your business. With over 2 billion users, it’s a great way to reach people where they already hang out—on their phones.

It’s all about sending personal, instant messages like texts, pictures, or videos. People open these messages almost every time (about 98%!), way more than email. You can use it to share deals, update orders, or help customers fast.

There are two tools: 

  • WhatsApp Business App: Free and simple, perfect for small businesses.
  • WhatsApp Business API: For bigger companies, with extras like automation and sending lots of messages at once.

To start, set up a business profile, ask customers to agree to get your messages (they have to say yes), and send friendly, useful stuff—not annoying spam. For example, a shop might text a discount to regulars, or an online store could remind someone about items left in their cart.

It works because it feels personal and meets people where they’re comfortable. Just keep it relevant and nice!
